# PossibleRecipes.md

**Agent workflows that compose ≥3 paid x402 routes into something worth more than the sum of its calls.**
All component prices are taken from real listed routes in `long-tail.csv` (median listed price per niche). Every recipe is *buildable today* from routes already in the catalog — what doesn't exist is the orchestrator.

Format: `Name ($total)` → `call ($) + call ($) + ...` → what it replaces → punchline.

## Meta-pattern (this is the whole pitch in one sentence)

Look at the totals. Every great recipe has the same shape:

> **5–10 cheap paid calls** → chained into one workflow → replaces a **$50–$2000/mo subscription** or a **$200/hr human** → executes in **30 seconds** → costs **under $2**.

That's the **knowledge-work compression curve** for actions, lookups, and decisions — the same arc LLMs are riding for text gen, but for verbs, not nouns.

The product pitch writes itself:

> *"Every SaaS subscription you pay monthly is a future x402 recipe.
> We mapped the 13,517 ingredients. Now we're building the chef."*

- **Tier S** is the deck.
